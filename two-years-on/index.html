Ouro’s Birthday Party

Ouro turns two this year! Come and celebrate with us in London, UK on 17th September 2014, 6pm onwards at Crypt on the Green (near Farringdon, London).

We would like to invite anyone who develops for, uses or is considering using Event Store. If you’re trying to convince your colleagues or management of the awesomeness of Event Store this is a great opportunity to bring them along and speak with our team in person.

Get your free ticket now!

Who’s speaking:

Greg Young (@gregyoung) will be presenting Event Store 3.0.0 and its new features. We’ll also have some short talks from some of our most prolific open-source community members, including:

  • Szymon Pobiega (@szymonpobiega)
  • João Bragança (@thefringeninja)
  • Jérémie Chassaing (@thinkb4coding)
  • Stanislavas Didenko (@valdasraps / @cern)

There will be plenty of time after these brief talks to chat with the speakers and other attendees. We’ll have plenty of food and drink to keep you energised throughout the night, and maybe even a few giveaways!

We’re hosting the evening at the Crypt on the Green, a short walk from either Farringdon or Angel tube stations. Check out these directions if you’re not sure where to go.
