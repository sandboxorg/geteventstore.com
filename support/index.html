Standard Support

Enterprise Support

Enhanced support packages are available for customers with more demanding requirements.

  • 24×7 availability.
  • Up to two hour response time for Priority 1 incidents.
  • Named account manager.

Community Support

The Event Store Google Group offers community-led support for customers with non-critical support requirements.
